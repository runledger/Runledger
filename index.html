Control the chaos of probabilistic software.

RunLedger is the CI harness for tool-using agents. Record tool calls once, replay in CI, and gate PRs with hard contracts, budgets, and baselines.

Not an eval metrics framework. Keep DeepEval for scoring; RunLedger makes agent tests deterministic and merge-gated.

Regression Blocking

Catch drift before it costs you.

LLMs are probabilistic, but your tool interfaces are strict. RunLedger enforces hard contracts (schema, tool order, budgets) and gates PRs against known baselines.

  • Baseline diffs

    Compare tool calls and outputs to a promoted baseline and fail on regressions.

  • Contract enforcement

    Fail the build if schema, tool order, or budgets are violated.

RunLedger vs DeepEval

Scoring and CI gates are different jobs. RunLedger is the harness for deterministic CI.

EVAL METRICS

DeepEval (Confident AI)

Evaluation metrics and model-graded scoring for quality and benchmarking.

  • Faithfulness, relevancy, and other scoring suites

RunLedger
